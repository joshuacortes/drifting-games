The Acura RSX, also known as the Honda Integra DC5 in some markets, is another popular platform for modification, with the most sought-after years being the initial production from 2001 to 2006. The RSX offers a sleek coupe design and a front-wheel-drive layout, making it a favorite among sport compact enthusiasts. Enthusiasts commonly opt for engine enhancements, suspension upgrades, and exterior modifications to enhance both its aesthetics and performance. The RSX's sporty nature and the availability of the high-performance Type-S variant make it a top choice for those seeking an engaging and stylish modified car.
